Meet Young Ventriloquist Brynn Cummings, The Next Darci Lynne With a Twist of Magic

Publish date: 2024-08-16

Photo by: Trae Patton/NBC

Reading Time: 3 minutes

Prepare to be amazed by the incredible talents of Brynn Cummings, an award-winning 11-year-old ventriloquist and magician hailing from Michigan. With an enchanting blend of magic tricks and ventriloquism, Brynn has captured the hearts of audiences worldwide. As she competes in Season 18 of America’s Got Talent, her journey is poised to leave a lasting impact on the world of entertainment.

Brynn Cummings is a Talented Ventriloquist With a Knack For Magic

Brynn’s magical journey began at the age of eight, when she received a puppet as a birthday gift. Little did she know that this humble present would ignite a lifelong passion for ventriloquism and the art of illusion. Eager to explore her newfound fascination, Brynn joined her local library’s magic club and quickly honed her skills, captivating audiences with her performances.

Her dedication and natural talent have garnered well-deserved recognition. In 2020, Brynn achieved first place at the International Brotherhood of Magicians competition, marking her as a rising star in the magic community. Inspired by the success of Darci Lynne, the winner of AGT, Brynn embarked on her own AGT journey, showcasing her mesmerizing stage presence and winning the hearts of both judges and viewers alike.

For those eager to witness Brynn’s enchanting journey on AGT Season 18, make sure to tune in to NBC every Tuesday night at 8/7c or catch up on her awe-inspiring performances online. Additionally, a special Watch Party will take place at Red Arrow Park in Paw Paw, Michigan, allowing fans to come together and share the excitement of watching Brynn’s captivating performances in the company of friends.

Beyond her live performances, Brynn extends her magical reach through her vibrant YouTube channel, where she shares not only her performances but also imparts valuable insights and tips for aspiring magicians.

She Has Gained Recognition Even Before AGT

Before captivating audiences on AGT, Brynn had already left a mark in the world of magic and ventriloquism. Despite her young age, her list of achievements is nothing short of extraordinary, showcasing her exceptional talent and passion for her craft.

In 2018, she claimed the coveted first-place title for the stage competition at the illustrious 82nd Annual Abbott’s Magic Get Together. Held in Colon, Michigan, fondly known as the “Magic Capital of the World,” this competition served as a launching pad for Brynn’s magical journey.

Building on her early success, Brynn continued to shine on the grand stage of ventriloquism. In 2022, she conquered the Junior Competition at the esteemed International Ventriloquist Convention (VentHaven), solidifying her position as a rising star in the world of ventriloquism.

Brynn’s prowess in the world of magic and ventriloquism caught the attention of renowned magicians Penn and Teller, leading to an invitation to appear on their television special. In an episode of Try This at Home Too, aired on the CW Network, Brynn showcased her skills and left audiences spellbound with her captivating performances.
