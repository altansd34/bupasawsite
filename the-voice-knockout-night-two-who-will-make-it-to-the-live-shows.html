The Voice Knockouts continue tonight, as the coaches will once again pair up their artists for Season 20. These performances will determine who makes it to the live shows, and who will be eliminated.

‘The Voice’ Knockout Predictions

During the Knockouts, two artists from each team face off, with each singer performing their own song. Their coach will choose one of them to move on. Each coach also has one chance to steal an artist from another team. Snoop Dogg will return this week as a Mega Mentor.

1. Zae Romeo vs. Rachel Mac (Team Nick)

Rachel is set to perform “Foolish Games” by Jewel, while Zae has chosen to sing “Electric Love” by Børns. Both of these artists won their respective Battles, so neither of them has the advantage of being on the team longer.

Zae turned all four chairs in his Blind Audition, while Rachel only turned coach Nick Jonas’ chair. Which one will Jonas go with? We predict he’ll choose Zae Romeo, but we wouldn’t be surprised if one of the other coaches steals Rachel for their own team.

2. Avery Roberson vs. Kenzie Wheeler (Team Kelly)

Avery chose to sing “Tomorrow” by Chris Young for his Knockout performance, while Kenzie will be performing “Beer Never Broke My Heart” by Luke Combs. Kenzie is an original Team Kelly member, while Avery was stolen from Team Blake in the Battles.

Both artists were four-chair turns in their Blind Auditions, so neither of them has an advantage there. It may all come down to these performances, but we predict Kelly Clarkson will stick with Kenzie Wheeler, whom she turned for almost immediately in the Blinds — while blocking Blake Shelton.

3. Rio Doyle vs. Zania Alaké (Team Legend)

Rio chose to perform “Issues” by Julia Michaels for this Knockout. Zania will be singing “If I Were Your Woman” by Gladys Knight. Both artists won their respective Battles, with neither of them being stolen from another team.

John Legend turned for both singers very quickly in the Blinds. For Rio, he was the only coach to turn. It could all come down to these Knockout performances, but we predict Legend will choose to keep Zania Alaké on his team for the live shows.

4. Connor Christian vs. Cam Anthony (Team Blake)

Connor will be singing “Youngblood” by 5 Seconds of Summer for his Knockout performance. Cam chose to sing “Feeling Good” by Nina Simone. Both artists won their respective Battles, and were original members of Team Blake.

Once again, it could all come down to this Knockout. Connor has a gritty voice that’s more typical of Shelton’s team, but we predict he’ll go out of the box and keep Cam Anthony on his team for the live shows.

5. Raine Stern vs. Jose Figueroa Jr. (Team Nick)

Jose chose to sing “Break Every Chain” by Tasha Cobbs for his Knockout performance. Raine will be singing “No Such Thing” by John Mayer. Both Raine and Jose won their respective Battles and are original Team Nick artists.

It will likely be a tough decision for Jonas, but he seemed glad to hear that Raine will be playing guitar for her performance. We predict he’ll go with Raine Stern’s unique talent as we move into the live shows.

6. Anna Grace vs. Gihanna Zoë (Team Kelly)

This Knockout was released ahead of Monday night’s show, so we’ve already seen both artists’ performances. Anna sang “If I Die Young” by The Band Perry, while Gihanna performed “Glitter in the Air” by P!nk.

Both singers are original Team Kelly members who won their Battles. Clarkson told Anna that her performance was “spiritual,” while she said Gihanna had “such a big presence.” We predict that she will choose Gihanna Zoë as the winner of this Knockout, but we wouldn’t be surprised if Shelton steals Anna for his team.

Who Will Win ‘The Voice’ Four-Way Knockout?

The four artists who were saved by their coaches in the Voice Battles will go up against each other in a Four-Way Knockout, to be voted on by the viewers. Only one of the four artists will make it into the live shows.

Devan Blake Jones (Team Nick) is singing “Sign of the Times” by Harry Styles, Savanna Woods (Team Kelly) is singing “Black Hole Sun” by Soundgarden, Emma Caroline (Team Blake) is singing “Neon Moon” by Brooks & Dunn, and Carolina Rial (Team Legend) is singing “Anyone” by Demi Lovato.

Considering this Knockout will be voted on by fans, we’re looking at how many views the artists’ auditions got. Carolina Rial has over a million hits on hers, so we’re predicting she’ll win the Four-Way Knockout.
