JoJo Siwa Launches Inclusive Pride Merchandise, Available May 1

JoJo Siwa is set to spread a message of love, acceptance, and inclusivity with the launch of her new merchandise. The collection features a Pride-themed line that will be available for purchase on May 1, 2023. This collection includes three unique lines designed by Siwa herself.

JoJo Siwa Announce New Pride Merchandise Collection

The 19-year-old singer, dancer, and social media personality, has revealed her latest venture that has left her fans thrilled. Siwa plans to launch a new merchandise collection that includes a line with the theme of Pride.

Siwa’s merchandise collection is designed to be creative, high-quality, and inclusive, catering to everyone. She stated that she has put everything she has into this merchandise to make sure it was perfect for everyone. Siwa’s new pride merch will arrive on Monday, May 1, a whole month ahead of Pride Month aka June.

Siwa has been an advocate for the LGBTQ+ community since she came out as a member of the community in January 2021. Since then, she has been using her fame to spread a message of love, acceptance, and inclusivity. With this move, she is continuing to spread that message through her merchandise collection.

Fans of Siwa and the LGBTQ+ community can visit JoJoSiwaPride.com to sign up for updates and purchase the collection when it launches. The launch of this merchandise collection is an exciting new venture for Siwa and a step towards greater inclusivity and acceptance.

JoJo Siwa Defends LGBTQ+ Rights By Shutting Down Candace Owens’ Homophobic Comments

Siwa has defended LGBTQ+ rights by shutting down Candace Owens’ absurd and homophobic comments on her sexuality. Owens, a pro-Trump activist, went on a rant on Siwa podcast in early April. Within the podcast, she claimed that Siwa isn’t gay, only seeking attention.

Many people widely criticized Owens’ comments, pointing out that deciding someone’s sexual orientation is not up to others.

“I haven’t watched the video yet, but if it has anything to do with your caption, respectfully back the f*ck off,” Siwa Tweeted.

Many people have widely celebrated Siwa’s advocacy for the LGBTQ+ community, while also praising her for utilizing her platform to promote acceptance. By shutting down Owens’ homophobic comments, Siwa has once again shown her commitment to fighting for the rights of all individuals, regardless of their sexual orientation or gender identity.
