Hayley Erbert is Hilariously Flexing Her Engagement Ring on Instagram

Publish date: 2024-08-29

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment Celebrity

Photo by Steven Simione/Getty Images, Hayley Erbert via Instagram

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Dancing With The Stars troupe member Hayley Erbert has found the funniest way to brag about her engagement ring. She is set to marry professional dancer Derek Hough, possibly this year.

Hayley Erbert Flexes Her Engagement Ring

Erbert is practically enjoying her time wearing her engagement ring from fiancé Derek Hough. In a recently uploaded video Instagram, she can be seen slowly opening a laptop using a hand where her ring can largely be noticed. Evidently so, the dancer was simply showing her appreciation towards Hough’s special gift.

“A lot of you have been asking for a tutorial of how to open my laptop so here it is,” she wrote. “Side note: good job, Derek Hough! I’m a little obsessed.”

In the comments, Hough revealed that he didn’t immediately catch the joke. Rather asking himself why Erbert was taking too slow opening the device. He nevertheless commented once more, saying that the ring was “flawless.”

Fans Are Keeping The Joke Alive

Completely getting Erbert’s agenda, fans took their time to play with the dancer. One follower even asked for a tutorial on how to close her laptop.

“I totally understand how to open a laptop now. Super helpful!” one wrote.

It’s not yet clear whether Hough and Erbert will marry this year. Nonetheless, earlier this month, the Dancing With The Stars judge shared that they are currently busy planning the perfect wedding.

“This year is going to be extremely busy, we’re just looking for a venue right now, so any suggestions?” Hough said. “We have a whole Pinterest and Instagram, you know, ideas, vision boards.”

Hough and Erbert first met in 2014 and began their romance in 2015. Believing that it’s already time to take their relationship to the next level. Thus, the decided to get engaged in June 2022.
