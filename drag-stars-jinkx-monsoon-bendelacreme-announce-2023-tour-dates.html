Drag Stars Jinkx Monsoon, BenDeLaCreme Announce 2023 Tour Dates

Publish date: 2024-08-29

Excitement is in the air as the tour dates for the sixth annual Jinkx & DeLa Holiday Show, featuring the beloved stars from RuPaul’s Drag Race, Jinkx Monsoon, and BenDeLaCreme have been revealed. This year’s tour promises to be their biggest joint venture yet, spanning across Europe, the United States, and Canada.

Jinkx & DeLa Holiday Show Tour Dates Have Been Announced

Fans from 30 cities will have the opportunity to witness their enchanting performances, including a stop at the iconic Dolby Theatre in Los Angeles, renowned for hosting the prestigious Oscars ceremony each year. In an exclusive statement, Jinkx expresses their enthusiasm for the upcoming tour.

“It’s been a wild year, so let’s end it wildly. DeLa and I strive to entertain while we enlighten and enliven,” Jinkx said. “So add us to your holiday plans, or let us be your holiday plans — either way, you’re welcome to join us.”

This year’s Jinkx & DeLa Holiday Show will feature a brand-new show written by Jinkx and DeLa, promising a spectacular blend of fabulous spectacle, whip-smart comedy, new songs, and beloved favorites. DeLa, known for their talent behind the scenes as well, will also direct and produce the project for BenDeLaCreme Presents, in collaboration with Kevin Heard, Gus Lanza, Jin Moon, and promotion handled by AEG Presents Comedy Division.

Early ticket sales for the tour will begin on July 19 at 10 a.m. local time for the United States and Canada, and on July 26 at 10 a.m. BT for the United Kingdom. Tickets for the U.S. and Canada dates will go on sale on July 21 at 10 a.m. ET, while the U.K. dates will be available on July 28 at 10 a.m. BT. For more information and to also secure your spot at the 2023 Jinkx & DeLa Holiday Show, visit JinkxandDeLa.com.

The Tour Will Take Visit More Than 30 Cities

The Jinkx & DeLa Holiday Show 2023 tour will take them to various cities across the globe, spreading holiday joy and entertainment. The journey begins on November 12 at the Theatre Royal in Glasgow, U.K., followed by shows at iconic venues like The London Palladium on November 13 and Liverpool Olympia on November 14. They will then cross the Atlantic, making their way to Clowes Memorial Hall in Indianapolis, Indiana, on November 19, and the Taft Theatre in Cincinnati, Ohio, on November 20.

The holiday extravaganza continues with performances on November 25 at the FirstOntario Concert Hall in Hamilton, Ontario, and on November 26 at Shea’s Performing Arts Center in Buffalo, New York. They will then embark on a series of shows across the United States, stopping at prestigious venues such as Wang Theatre in Boston, Massachusetts, on November 28, and College Street Music Hall in New Haven, Connecticut, on November 29.

These are just a glimpse of the exciting tour dates, as Jinkx and DeLa will continue to spread their magic across the United States, with stops in Texas, California, Oregon, and beyond. The grand finale of their tour will take place on December 30 at the Orpheum in Vancouver, British Columbia, leaving fans in awe of the incredible performances they have witnessed throughout the holiday season.
