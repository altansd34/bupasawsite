America’s Got Talent Season 18 will come to a close on Wednesday night, as the winner is announced. As usual, the finale will also feature several guest performances. During Tuesday’s performance episode, host Terry Crews shared who fans can expect to see.

AGT Announces Finale Guest Performers

On Tuesday night, Crews teased that the Season 18 finale will feature “duets, mashups, and superstars.” Guest performers include musicians Jason Derulo, Jon Batiste, Diane Warren, Leona Lewis, and Thirty Seconds to Mars.

The episode will also feature appearances from celebrity chef Cat Cora, as well as last year’s AGT winners, the Mayyas. Traditionally, the show’s finalists take the stage with the guest performers, so it will be interesting to see who’s matched up.

It looks like Jon Batiste and Thirty Seconds to Mars pre-taped their performances for the finale earlier this week, as they were listed for a taping that took place on Monday. Batiste also posted about the performance on Instagram that day.

Wednesday’s results show will air at 9/8c and run for two hours, as we find out the results of the overnight vote. At the end of the night, the winner of Season 18 will be announced.

The Finalists Pulled Out All the Stops This Week

On Tuesday night, the Top 11 finalists for Season 18 delivered some incredible performances for their last chance to earn America’s vote. They made it very hard to decide who was most deserving of the $1 million prize.

The night included beautiful vocal performances from Golden Buzzers Putri Ariani and Lavender Darcangelo, as well as singing groups 82nd Airborne Division Chorus and Mzansi Youth Choir. Three dance groups (Avantgardey, Chibi Unity, and Murmuration) also delivered showstopping routines.

But it wasn’t all about song and dance. Magician Anna DeGuzman pulled off a truly amazing card trick, while Ahren Belisle made everyone laugh. Adrian Stoica & Hurricane proved why they might be the best dog act in the show’s history, and Ramadhani Brothers had everyone on the edge of their seat.

Tune in to Wednesday night’s results show on NBC to find out which of these acts will be named the Season 18 winner.
