7 Stunning Celebrities Who Are Still Single and Their Fans Keep Wondering Why

Publish date: 2024-08-10

It might seem like celebrities have it all — money, fast cars, beautiful houses. Yet, you can never guess when it comes to matters of love. Some celebs are happy to find the sweetheart of their life, while others can’t find anyone, no matter how hard they try. Even the rich and famous don’t have any insurance for heartbreaks.

1. Angelina Jolie

zz/RE/Westcom/STAR MAX/IPx/Associated Press/East News

This gorgeous actress hasn’t been seeing anyone for a long time. Although fans made some assumptions, no official information has ever been given.

Once Angelina was seen dining with The Weeknd in July 2021. Rumors about their possible relationship spread immediately. Yet, no romantic feelings were ever involved. They’re just good friends.

2. Jason Momoa

Elizabeth Goodenough/Everett Collection/East News

This handsome Hawaiian actor isn’t committed to anyone either. He broke up with Eiza González several weeks after they officially announced their relationship. Jason’s busy schedule and his aspiration to become more successful were the reasons for their separation.

3. Chris Evans

zz/Dennis Van Tine/STAR MAX/IPx/Associated Press/East News

The sexiest man alive wants to stay single until he finds “the one.” He wants a person who has their own interests and goals in life. The actor also admitted his fear of commitment, and he’s not ready to trade his independence for a suffocating relationship.

4. Halle Berry

VALERIE MACON/AFP/East News

The actress has been married 3 times, yet, still prefers being single. Halle loves spending time with her kids, she also wants to quit dating completely and appreciate her me-time more. Berry doesn’t think it’s necessary to have a relationship anymore. If she finally finds her perfect match, great, if not, no big deal.

5. Demi Lovato

face to face/FaceToFace/REPORTER/East News

Demi has been single after breaking up with Max Ehrich. They got engaged and broke up 2 months later. The singer says, “The best part about being single is knowing that my happiness is coming from no other person than myself.”

6. Emilia Clarke

face to face/FaceToFace/REPORTER/East News

Emilia hasn’t had any relationships since her breakup with film director Charlie McDowell. The actress even tried using the celeb dating app, Raya. Shortly after, she deleted her profile because it wasn’t what she hoped it would be. Let’s wait and see, maybe we can finally see the moment when Emilia and Jason Momoa decide to date.

7. Charlize Theron

Jordan Strauss/Invision/East News

It’s hard to wrap our heads around the fact that this bombshell actress is still single. Charlize is focused on her career and children, and this is what makes her happy. She openly states that she never wanted to get married, but at the same time, she doesn’t mind dating.

How difficult do you think it is to balance your professional and personal life? What matters more to you — career or love?
