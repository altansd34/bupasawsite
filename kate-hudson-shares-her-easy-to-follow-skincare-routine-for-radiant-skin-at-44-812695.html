Kate Hudson Shares Her Easy-to-Follow Skincare Routine for Radiant Skin at 44

Publish date: 2024-08-25

At 44, Kate Hudson’s skin is nothing short of glowing and radiant. According to the award-winning actress, it all starts with a mindful approach to skincare. She emphasizes the importance of using gentle, natural skincare products and avoiding harsh chemicals that can damage the skin. By adopting Kate Hudson’s simple yet effective skincare tips, you too can achieve healthy, radiant, and youthful-looking skin at any age.

She doesn’t wash her face in the morning.

The actress suggests that over-washing your face is unnecessary and she doesn’t use any cleansers in her morning skincare routine. By avoiding harsh cleansing products, she maintains a healthy and glowing complexion.

“I don’t ever wash my face in the morning,” she said. “I just wash my face with water. I find that my face gets too stripped when I’m washing it too much—it gets all dull and weird. You know, water does a good job, and the washcloth is a little exfoliating,” she added.

She washes her face twice at night.

While the Glass Onion star prefers to maintain a minimalistic morning skincare routine, she devotes more time and energy to effectively cleanse her skin after a long day. Her skincare practice emphasizes the significance of incorporating a thorough cleansing routine into your skincare regimen to eliminate impurities and pollutants that accumulate throughout the day.

“At night I wash my face twice. I also facial-steam at night, but I don’t steam every day. I don’t think that’s good for your skin. I steam maybe two, three times a week,” she shared.

She is passionate about clean beauty.

The skin is the largest organ in our body, and anything we put on it can have an impact on our overall health. Hudson acknowledges that she hadn’t always been mindful of the ingredients in the skincare products she used. However, after learning about the potential dangers of certain chemicals like parabens, she shifted to a more conscious approach.

“No one had ever told me what was in my products. I decided, whatever I can control, I’m going to change the way that I approach it. And so skin care and makeup is number one,” she said.

She does low-impact workouts.

Apart from making you feel invigorated, regular workouts can promote skin detoxification, prevent premature aging, and enhance your complexion’s radiance. According to Hudson, you don’t necessarily have to sweat it out at the gym to experience these benefits for your skin.

“I don’t believe in overexertion. I like low impact, I do a lot of functional movement. I like my yoga, I like my pilates, and I walk every day. I’m a take-the-stairs-don’t-take-the-elevator kind of person. Our bodies need to be working, but you don’t necessarily need to be running from tigers,” she said.
