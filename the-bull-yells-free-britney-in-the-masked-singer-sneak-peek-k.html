In a new sneak peek of The Masked Singer Season 6, the Bull delivers a powerhouse performance of “Circus” by Britney Spears. Fans are loving the clip, particularly because of the “Free Britney” message contained in it.

Fans are also offering their guesses about who could be under the mask. We’ll have to wait until we have more clues to be sure, but could viewers have already figured this one out before the show airs?

The Bull Sings Britney Spears on ‘The Masked Singer’

The Bull starts his performance on a raised platform in the center of the stage, as if he’s the ringleader of a circus. He’s surrounded by dancers dressed, appropriately, like matadors. The Bull totally owns the stage as he performs the song, at one point telling the audience to “make some noise.”

Before a dance break, the Bull even says, “Free Britney” into the microphone. This is a reference to the movement to end Spears’ conservatorship, which began in 2008. Fans are loving that the Bull gave a shoutout to the movement while performing one of Spears’ most iconic songs.

The Bull also has some seriously impressive dance skills. Not only does he make the most of his flowing cape, but he also does a split at one point! The judges are clearly wowed by his talent, with Jenny McCarthy asking, “Who is this guy?!”

Who Is Under the Bull Mask This Season?

It seems pretty clear from the Bull’s first performance that he has singing and dancing experience. And if you glance at the replies to the sneak peek on Twitter, several fans seem pretty confident about who’s under that mask.

The consensus seems to be that the Bull is YouTuber, Broadway star, and former American Idol contestant Todrick Hall. As one Twitter user wrote, “That is ten thousand million percent Todrick Hall. I’d recognize that voice anywhere.”

Another user suggested that judge Nicole Scherzinger should be able to recognize Hall’s voice, since they’ve recorded together. Someone else called this a “great opportunity” for people to see Hall’s talent, saying he’s underrated.

Do you agree with fans? Is Todrick Hall under the Bull mask? Let us know in the comments, and tune in to the Season 6 premiere of The Masked Singer on Wednesday, September 22, on FOX.
