Angelica Hale’s name is very fitting because her voice is nothing short of angelic. The America’s Got Talent alum just dropped her new cover of “Set a Place at Your Table.” She sounds so amazing to the point that it feels like she is singing an original song. Justin Bieber fans know this is an original song of his from 14 years ago.

‘AGT’ Star Angelica Hale Delivers Moving Justin Bieber Cover

Bieber had originally recorded “Set a Place at Your Table” to help raise money for a local food bank in 2007. It was a part of a CD released at Christmas time and only sold in local stores. Angelica’s spin on the song is very moving. The way she delivers impactful lyrics about helping those in need is truly inspiring. She’s using YouTube as a platform to deliver the message to her 2.74 million subscribers.

In the music video, Angelica belts the song while sitting at a piano. We see a montage of Angelica setting the table for a family dinner. She invites her neighbors to come and join, echoing the sentiment of the song about setting a place at the table for people who might need it the most. Angelica has risen to fame since her time on AGT Season 12 and still has the same huge heart.

“Wow, Angelica. This song reached right inside my chest and wrapped a hand around my heart,” one fan wrote in a YouTube comment. “I think this is one of the most powerful songs you’ve ever given us. Thank you so much for that. Your delivery was breathtaking, and the lyrics were so meaningful. Keep shining, you bright young star.”

Angelica has grown one of the biggest fanbases out of all of the AGT contestants throughout the show’s 16 seasons. She recently covered Calum Scott’s “You Are The Reason.” The clip now has over 40,000 views on YouTube. “Set a Place at Your Table” is now available on all streaming platforms. The 14-year-old AGT star already has over 1 million accumulated streams on Spotify and counting.
