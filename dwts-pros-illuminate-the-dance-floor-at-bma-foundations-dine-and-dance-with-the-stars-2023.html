'DWTS' Pros Illuminate The Dance Floor At BMA Foundation's Dine and Dance With The Stars 2023

Publish date: 2024-08-04

Photo by: ABC/Eric McCandless

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Kansas City, Missouri was swept away by a wave of mesmerizing talent as the Dancing with the Stars professionals graced the stage at the 2023 BMA Foundation Dinner. This star-studded event, held at the Sheraton Crown Center, brought together a constellation of dance virtuosos. The professionals captivated the audience with their grace, passion, and sheer artistry filled performances.

DWTS Pros Perform at The BMA Foundation Dinner

The evening of dancing began with a burst of energy, as Hayley Erbert and Artem Chigvintsev ignited the stage with a captivating display of precision. Their seamless chemistry and intricate choreography left the audience absolutely spellbound.

Following Erbert and Chigvintsev was former real life couple, Emma Slater and Sasha Farber. The two certainly left a trail of elegance and grace. Their performance was a symphony of fluid movements and also precise footwork, each step telling a story of its own. The duo’s seamless synchronization certainly showcased their exceptional skill, leaving the audience in awe of their remarkable talent.

Later, Britt Stewart and Gleb Savchenko took the stage by storm, infusing the room with infectious energy and unbridled charisma. Their high-energy routine commanded attention, as they then effortlessly glided across the floor, their smiles lighting up the room.

The final group performance was definitely a feast for the senses, an unforgettable performance of collective artistry. The DWTS pros united their talents, creating a seamless tapestry of movement that painted the stage with vibrant colors. Their precise formations and dynamic choreography showcased the unity and camaraderie among the dancers, leaving the audience breathless and yearning for more.

What is The BMA Foundation Dine and Dance With The Stars?

The Brian Anselmo Memorial (BMA) Foundation holds an annual dinner event named Dine and Dance With the Stars in Kansas City, Missouri. It serves as a fundraiser for local charities supported by the BMA Foundation. The event features a dinner, a silent auction, and also a dance competition involving local celebrities. The proceeds raised from the auction and dance competition then go towards supporting the foundation’s mission and the chosen charities.

In addition to the fundraising activities, the BMA Foundation Dinner also includes an awards ceremony to recognize individuals who have made significant contributions to supporting the foundation’s mission. The awards ceremony seems to have a themed element as well.

The event aims to bring the community together for a night of entertainment, dining, and fundraising, all in support of local charities. If you have an interest in attending this year’s BMA Foundation Dinner, mark your calendar for the event at the Sheraton Crown Center. The event will take place on June 11 at 6 p.m.
