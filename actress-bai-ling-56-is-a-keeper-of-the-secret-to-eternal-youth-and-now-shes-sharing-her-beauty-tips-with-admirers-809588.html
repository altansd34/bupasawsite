Actress Bai Ling, 56, Is a Keeper of the Secret to Eternal Youth, and Now Shes Sharing Her Beauty

Publish date: 2024-08-22

Bai Ling is an actress who, apart from her acting talent, is famous for her extreme extravagance in everything she does and says. Once, in an interview, she claimed that she was from the Moon, where her grandmother lives. But the truly astonishing thing that makes people continue to talk about her is her never-fading appearance that she’s managed to maintain throughout the years. Our article will lift the veil on Bai Ling’s personal secrets to how she keeps her body and spirit as young as ever.

Bai Ling has a lot of concerns about how her image is presented in the press.

Allstar / Graham Whitby Boot / Mary Evans Picture Library / East News

Bai Ling is a very popular Chinese-American actress. She is famous for her roles in The Crow, Red Corner, Crank: High Voltage, and Dumplings, as well as TV shows, like Entourage and Lost. She even won Best Supporting Actress at the 2004 Hong Kong Film Awards and the Golden Horse Awards for her role in Dumplings.

Despite all her fame and recognition, Ling is worried about the false image that Hollywood has produced for her. In one of her interviews, she said, “I accidentally or innocently destroyed the beautiful Bai Ling that everybody loved, that beautiful, talented actress. Instead, the media brought me out as this crazy woman showing her body everywhere. I become this character the pop culture Hollywood machine created. Somehow, I become a victim of that image.”

Now the actress devotes more time to herself and has been rewarded with a really youthful image.

Chris Pizzello / Invision / AP / East News

EVERETT COLLECTION / EAST NEWS

Nowadays, Bai Ling is best known and admired for her outrageous fashion sense and the ageless looks that her fans never get tired of commenting on.

Recently, the actress shared some throwback photos of herself on her Instagram page, and these pics appeared in the spotlight. She captioned these shots with a heartfelt message to all her fans.

She wrote, “Time changes us, I would say we are just evolved, just a different kind of us, different feelings of us, in different romance with nature, with life. Don’t judge ourselves, just embrace, because it is just like life, like four seasons, there are beauties everywhere in each of us in different times, in different seasons, but all with beauty.”

Bai Ling has a special view of what it means to take care of herself and her beauty.

In a interview dedicated to her role in Dumplings, Ling honestly shared her approach to her beauty routine. The interviewer asked the actress, “The main topic of the film is beauty. The women in the film battle against the loss of it. What is beauty for you?”

And Ling didn’t hesitate to answer, saying, “For me, nature is beauty. To live in the moment is beauty. It’s the soul of the person, which is beauty. You have to accept the change of nature. It’s a circle, the whole life is a circle. I don’t have fears of becoming old. It’s okay to become old, to get wrinkles. It’s a part of life. You have to accept yourself, be yourself. Then you are beautiful. I don’t do anything for my beauty. I don’t trust things like cosmetics. They don’t have any use.”

She also has her own “beauty and health theory.” On her official website, she wrote, “I really don’t do any exercise, you live, that’s exercise naturally. And I really eat a lot of desserts because that’s what I like to eat, a joy for me, but the key to keeping your body healthy is to think healthy. Our thoughts are powerful because your mind is your central station that controls the entire universe of your body, see if you think healthy, then your body will function smoothly in its natural rhythm.”

And she added, “You know we all choose healthy food to eat, but we forgot the simple yet most important element in our life, which is to select healthy and good thoughts to think.”
