'AGT' Finalists Mzansi Youth Choir Performs An Intimate Rendition of 'Fix You' Alongside Coldplay

Publish date: 2024-08-04

TALENTRECAP.COM Talent America's Got Talent

Photo by:Trae Patton/NBC

Reading Time: 2 minutes

The Mzansi Youth Choir became a big hit and fan favorite on Season 18 of America’s Got Talent. They even won the first ever Audience Golden Buzzer, and their journey led to a thrilling collaboration with the famous band Coldplay.

Mzansi Youth Choir Performed in Coldplay’s Concert

The Mzansi Youth Choir hails from Soweto, a township of Johannesburg, South Africa. Founded two decades ago, this choir serves as a safe haven for young talents seeking to develop their skills and share their passion for music. Their journey on AGT Season 18 was nothing short of inspiring, capturing the hearts of both the judges and the viewers.

The most amazing part? The audience loved their performance so much that they gave the Mzansi Youth Choir the Golden Buzzer themselves. It was a special moment, showing just how much their performance meant to everyone.

Although the Mzansi Youth Choir did not ultimately win AGT, their journey did not end there. Following their elimination from the competition, the choir received an exciting opportunity to perform alongside the internationally acclaimed band, Coldplay.

On September 28, during Coldplay’s second night in San Diego, California, the Mzansi Youth Choir took the stage with Coldplay for a breathtaking collaboration. They performed Coldplay’s iconic song, “Fix You,” as well as one of their original songs, creating a magical and unforgettable musical experience for the audience.

The Choir Thanks AGT, Coldplay For The Memorable Performances

The Mzansi Youth Choir recently took to Instagram to express their gratitude and excitement for the unforgettable experiences they’ve had in the past few days. From their stunning performance in the AGT finale to sharing the stage with Coldplay in front of thousands of people, these moments have been nothing short of a dream come true for the talented South African choir.

In their heartfelt Instagram post, the choir shared their gratitude for the professionals they’ve had the privilege to work with, including the members of Coldplay. They described these professionals as nothing but supportive and considerate, highlighting the collaborative spirit that made these performances so special. The choir’s post was filled with appreciation for everyone who played a part in making these incredible opportunities a reality.

Notably, AGT host Terry Crews chimed in with a comment on the post, expressing his pride in the choir. His comment reflects the admiration and support the Mzansi Youth Choir has garnered not only from the audience but also from the AGT family and beyond.
