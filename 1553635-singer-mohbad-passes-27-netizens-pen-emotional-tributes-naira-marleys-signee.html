Shock, Tears As Mohbad Dies at 27, Netizens Pen Tributes to Former Naira Marleys Signee

Publish date: 2024-08-04
  • Singer Mohbad, who was formerly signed to Naira Marley's Marlian music label, has passed on at 27
  • The news of Mohbad's death was confirmed by journalist Ovie on Twitter, which stirred emotions online
  • Details surrounding Mohbad's death remain unknown as netizens took to social media to pen touching tributes to the singer

Nigerian musician Ilerioluwa Oladimeji Aloba, known professionally as Mohbad, has passed on at 27.

Popular entertainment journalist Ovie confirmed the singer's death in a post on his X (formerly Twitter) page.

It was revealed that the 27-year-old Nigerian rapper, singer and songwriter passed away on Tuesday, September 12, 2023.

As of the time of this report, the circumstances surrounding his death remain unknown.
