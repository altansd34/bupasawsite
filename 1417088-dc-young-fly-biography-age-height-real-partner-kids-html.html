Career

In 2015, he became a recurring cast member on MTV2's improv comedy show titled Wild 'N Out (Season 7). Later, he was named "Rookie of the Year" of Season 7.

Apart from improv comedy shows, he also tried acting. He made his film debut in the Christmas comedy-drama film Almost Christmas (2016), where he played a minor role.

The same year, he made an appearance in the film #DigitalLivesMatter. Following that, he landed numerous roles in films and television shows.

Aside from acting and improv comedy, he also tried producing. As a producer, he worked on the BET Hip-Hop Awards show (2020) and the film #DigitalLivesMatter (2016).

He also pursued a career as a musician in addition to acting. He began rapping in 2011.

He made his debut in 2014 with the single Westside Connection, which was quickly followed by M.A.P. He then released No Weed and Right Now in 2016. Following that, he took a short break to host the revival of Total Request Live (TRL) on MTV (2017–2018).
