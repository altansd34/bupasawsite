What 15 Mature Singers Looked Like When They Were Younger

Publish date: 2024-08-10

While we might not like the idea, time certainly doesn’t stop for anyone. From one day to the next, we wake up and realize how fast we have grown older. And when it comes to celebrities, it works the exact same way. That’s right, icons like Cher or Paul McCartney age as well. But even if some of the biggest stars of all time have now turned 70 or even older, no matter how old they are, they’re still celebrities, and we’ll love them until the end of time.
