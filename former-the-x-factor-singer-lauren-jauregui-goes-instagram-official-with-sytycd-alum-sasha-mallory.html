Lauren Jauregui, a former member of the hit girl group Fifth Harmony, has recently made headlines after going Instagram official with Sasha Mallory. The couple had been rumored to be dating for some time, but Jauregui’s latest post on the social media platform confirmed their relationship and set the internet abuzz with excitement.

Lauren Jauregui Confirms Relationship With Sasha Mallory

Jauregui’s decision to make her relationship with Mallory Instagram official is a significant moment, not only for the couple but for their fans as well. Like many other couples out there, both the former X Factor singer and So You Think You Can Dance competitor posted their celebration on social media.

The post, which features a series of romantic photos of the couple, was accompanied by a simple but heartfelt caption in which Jauregui expressed her love for Mallory. In the uploaded pictures, the couple can be seen posing at events, holding hands, and taking selfies all over the place.

Mallory, who is a professional dancer and choreographer, has also shared photos of herself with Jauregui on her own Instagram account. She has appeared on stage with Jauregui as well as in the live-streamed 2021 PRELUDE performance film.

Fans continue to celebrate Jauregui and Mallory’s relationship, many have commented on the natural chemistry between the two, noting how happy they seem to be together. Singer and songwriter Kehlani showed support for the pair in the comments along with MTV and many others.

Lauren Jauregui’s Stand On The LGBTQ+ Community

Jauregui has always been a vocal advocate for LGBTQ+ rights and has used her platform to promote acceptance and inclusivity. In a 2018 interview, she spoke about the importance of using her voice to raise awareness about issues affecting the community.

“It’s important for me to use my platform to speak out about the issues that affect me and the people I love,” Jauregui said. “I want to create a space where everyone feels safe and valued, regardless of who they are or who they love.”

The couple’s decision to share their relationship is just another example of their commitment to promoting love and acceptance. It is a reminder that love is love, and that everyone deserves to be able to express themselves and their feelings freely, without fear of judgment or discrimination.
