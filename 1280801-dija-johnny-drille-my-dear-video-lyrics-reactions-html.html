Dija and Johnny Drille - My Dear: video, lyrics, reactions

Publish date: 2024-08-30

Two of the most talented Mavin Records crew members have teamed up to unleash a fantastic number. The brand new banger by Di’ja and Johnny Drille - My Dear is the hottest thing in town that has got necks turning and everyone talking. It is a hit that you cannot afford to miss.

This new song comes as a follow up to Johnny's previous hit 'Count On You' which, according to his fans, has a colorful video. 'Count On You' was directed by the multi-talented Timmy Davies.

Di’ja and Johnny Drille - My Dear description

  • Release date: November 28th, 2019
  • Format: Video
  • Genre: RnB
  • Length: 3 minutes 26 seconds
  • Label: Mavin Records

It is a folk love song with the right message and lyrics. The song seeks for lovers with the same common interest to be together and not apart from each other. It is undoubtedly a hit that will settle misunderstandings, break ups and heal wounds.
